    Cholera Outbreak, Stockholm, 1853.

    Context

    A large Cholera outbreak in late August 1853 in Stockholm, Sweden. About 3% of the population died within a couple of weeks. Poor parts of the city, such as St Catherine parish was hit very hard.
    The church was responsible for all population registration until around 1870. and the data was collected from hand-written church records. Only death records have been analysed.

    Key Performance Indicators
  • Total deaths
  • Percentage of deaths by gender
  • Number of deaths by month
  • Number of deaths by weekday
  • Number of deaths by Age-group
